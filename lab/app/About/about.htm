About

My name is Simon Keefe and I am the Front‑End Web Developer at Australian Rugby Union in Sydney. I work with a great team to build a wide range of web‑based solutions for local club rugby all the way up to our national teams. It is awesome being able to use my skillset in an organisation I am passionate about.

My primary task involves working on Rugby.com.au to improve usability and ultimately increase traffic. I work closely with the Digital Media Director, assessing potential future enhancements and their priority. I am then responsible for the Front-End Development tasks in the sprint, working with a Backend Developer until the updates are deployed. I really enjoy my role as it gives me a great opportunity to gain experience making UX and IA descions in a high traffic site, whilst also continuing to build my technical skillset.

In addition, my role involves providing technical support for ARU's other sites, working with various sectors of the organisation to build and refine our digital presence.

Prior to my current role I was the Senior Front-End Developer at Cornerstone Digital. I really enjoyed my time here working with the team to create high quality web solutions for our clients.

Offline my wife and I are quickly learning how much energy is required to look after two children under two! It is exciting, and at times, taxing! We live in Sydney, spend a lot of time with friends and family and support the Wallabies!
